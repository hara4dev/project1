KASB'ART Rooftop

Juice bar & Restaurant Tangier Kasbah

Juice Bar

Spend some quality time with your family, friends, or even yourself and savor our natural exotic homemade juice while admiring the oriental vibes of our interiors, at the Kasb'art Rooftop.

We are located just a 5 minutes walk from Bab el Kasbah and close to the other tourist sites, don't hesitate to get a drink on the go from our juice bar. Here, our juice is made by our highly trained Baristas who offer an exquisite mix of exotic flavors and mediterranean fruits.

You can enjoy your drinks inside our cozy local and rooftop where we offer a variety of dishes as well. Breakfast, brunch, lunch, desserts are all available. feel free to try some of our freshly made delights, you will be caught in the trance of the oriental vibes.
